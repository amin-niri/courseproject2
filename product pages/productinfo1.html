The modern day watch is here!
Although electronic watches like this has been under development for many many years, this one might have some fate in becoming a big success. Apple has thrown in some creative features to attract users. With features such as walkie-talkie, sketching, heartbeat tracking, tapping, the iWatch seems like a fun toy which will open doors for many new apps to be made. It doesn't end there, the iWatch will keep track of your movements and heartbeat to give you feedback on your fitness. The main selling point of this device will be that it is easier to stay connected to the world, mainly your friends.
