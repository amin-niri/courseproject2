My perspective from all angles
This is one of the products we see today that is getting very popular very quickly. Drones are going to become a huge market in the coming years. We already see many of these drones being used to record videos, such as real estate 360 views of the homes realtors try to sell. I think you can accomplish many more ambitious things with drones such as what facebook is doing, trying to provide internet to countries that can't afford it with self-sustaining drones that spread internet. These machines are very cool and very exciting to see what they will be used for in the next few years.
