All Aboard the Electric Vehicle Bandwagon?
What happens when you fuse a luxury sedan and an electric car together?

You get a Tesla Model S!

The Model S is a powerhouse of a car compared to your average electric car. It is capable of going 0-60 mph in under 5 seconds! The overall look and feel of the vehicle's interior and exterior are also beautifully and expertly crafted together to give the driver and the passengers a remarkable experience.

At the centre of the Model S, Tesla has refitted the old, traditional style sound system and temperature control knobs with a tablet. With the tablet, countless adjustments can be made to the Model S with just a swipe of a finger! For example, the entire car can be raised or lowered, or set to adjust depending on the speed of the car. One of the more impressive features is the auto-updating feature. Tesla is able to push updates to the Model S while connected to the Wifi, which can instantly fix kinks and glitches within the car.

Currently, infrastructure (Supercharger stations) is mainly set up in the United States, so it is only really efficient to own a Model S there. With the supercharger stations, one is able to charge their car up fully in around 30 minutes when compared to a 2 hour charge at home! However, Tesla has plans to expand to all corners of North America. so hang tight!

With green innovations such as the Model S, we can definitely look towards a green future of driving. This product illustrates how computer science has been able to connect to the field of environmental sustainability. Considering the fact that vehicles are a globally significant component of everyone's lives, this product tackles this component and gives it a green makeover. With such technologies and merging of disciplines, one can be hopeful of a future that serves to conserve and sustain our natural surroundings and resources.
